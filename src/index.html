Objective

My primary goal is to create clean, cutting-edge, engaging interfaces which empower users to more easily and effectively perform complex tasks with ease and grace.

The web's potential for drawing and exploring meaningful relationships between the people, things, and ideas around us is unparalleled, unexplored, and bristling with possibility. The vital role of a developer is to ensure that a user's experience is one of discovery, joy, and the intuitive synthesis of ideas yet to be imagined.

Successful interactions are complex culminations of context, design, and accessibility—all of which hinge upon a developer's ability to be both empathetic and efficient. By doing my damndest to improve the usability and performance of applications and websites, I am constantly pushing myself to advance the state of the web.
